# Scene 1: The Seal Fails

## Setting
The world plunges into true "Nightfall" as the Seal actively fails.

## Key Events
- The Nastaku's influence spreads as the Box cracks further
- First signs of the final phase of the Eternal Game
- Brian begins to manifest his anomalous nature

## Characters Present
- Brian (beginning to understand his significance)
- Fenningway (monitoring the situation)
- Harker (sensing the opportunity)

## Purpose
Establish the critical state of the Seal and introduce the race between forces to claim the Black Swan.
