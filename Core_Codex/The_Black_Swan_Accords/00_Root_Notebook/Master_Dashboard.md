# The Black Swan Accords - Master Dashboard

## Grand Arc Overview

The Black Swan Accords represent the multiversal law and protocol established by the Ancient Starwalkers to manage the **incalculable anomaly**—the one being or event that could either end the long war or destroy existence entirely.

### The Original Covenant (The Accords)

The Accords are a set of meta-rules established after The Sundering that govern:

- **The Cycles**: Mandating the 33,000-year Epochs and the Return of the Starwalkers/Preori
- **Neutrality**: Requiring the Starwalkers to operate in shadows, guiding without direct interference
- **The Anomaly Clause**: Protocol for the Awakening and Protection of the **Black Swan**
