**Author’s Note / Mythic Context**

The Black Swan Accords chronicles the mythic, real, and interdimensional saga of Diana Swan, Hawk Eye, Robin Richardson, and the Armada—a cosmic tale of rebellion, sovereignty, and system reclamation. It merges real-world parallels and synchronicities with trans-dimensional myth.

- **Loyalty & Reliability** – True alliance forged through trials.

- **Micro-Cameos** – Small, real-life actions that ripple through the mythic web.

| Component | Purpose |
